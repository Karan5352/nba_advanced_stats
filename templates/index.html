Advanced Metrics Explained

Player Metrics:

  • PER (Player Efficiency Rating): A comprehensive per-minute rating that sums up all positive accomplishments and subtracts negative ones
  • True Shooting % (TS%): A measure of shooting efficiency that takes into account field goals, 3-point field goals, and free throws
  • Usage Rate (USG%): An estimate of the percentage of team plays used by a player while on the court

Team Metrics:

  • Offensive Rating (OFF_RTG): Points scored per 100 possessions
  • Defensive Rating (DEF_RTG): Points allowed per 100 possessions
  • Net Rating (NET_RTG): Point differential per 100 possessions (OFF_RTG - DEF_RTG)
